Mimosa Mocktail

Description

A delicious mocktail that is slightly sour in taste, and adults as well as kids will love!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup ice, or as needed
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 1/4 cup club soda, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar

Steps

  1. Fill a cocktail shaker halfway with ice
  2. Add orange juice and club soda
  3. Cover and shake until the outside of shaker has frosted
  4. Strain into a chilled champagne flute
  5. Stir in sugar and serve immediately
